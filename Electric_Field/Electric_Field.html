Electric Field

The electric field vector, E, is a quantity that we assign to a point in space. Given this quantity, we can compute the force on a charge Q will experience if it is placed at that point in space using the equation F=QE.

To find E at any point in space, compute the force F due to all other charges on a hypothetical (or ‚Äútest‚Äù) charge qo‚Äã at a point where you want to know E. To find E at that point, divide F by qo‚Äã.

E=qo‚ÄãF‚Äã
